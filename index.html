Maturity without ambiguity

One direction. Clearly separated stages.​

Every public capability is presented as a working foundation, V1-testnet target, research direction, or item requiring external validation.

Working foundation

Local AIA, governed retrieval and memory, encrypted local content foundations, owner-authorized devices, and local contribution simulations.

A production-candidate test environment intended to use test-only value, reviewed releases, and independently evidenced behavior.

Intelligent Chain verification and settlement, followed by post-V1 exploration of collective intelligence through GENOME.
